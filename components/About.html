About Indian Pilgrimage

Experience the spiritual and cultural richness of India through our virtual visit to the most popular pilgrimage sites in the country.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the beauty and sanctity of Indian pilgrimage sites to people around the world. Through our virtual visit, we aim to provide an immersive experience that enables visitors to connect with the spiritual and cultural essence of these places, without having to leave the comfort of their homes.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate individuals who believe in the transformative power of pilgrimage. Our team includes experts in history, culture, and spirituality, who have dedicated their lives to studying and experiencing the pilgrimage sites of India.

Our team is led by Swapnil and includes the following members: Shreya, Ram, Amey, Pradnya, and Vaibhav. Together, we have dedicated our lives to studying and experiencing the pilgrimage sites of India, bringing their spiritual and cultural essence to people around the world.
